NOTES
=====

1. Each SW1xxxx texture has a corresponding SW2xxxx texture,
   but only the SW1 texture name is listed above, the SW2
   counterpart is implied.

2. The "BURNING BARREL" thing (doomnum 70) can be used in
   Doom 1 levels, since the sprites exist in the IWAD, but
   the animation is not smooth (the barrel bounces left
   and right, probably due to wrong offset values).
